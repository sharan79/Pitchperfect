Pitch Perfect is about presenting ideas for solving problems in a range of domains. This aims to leverage design, thinking and innovative learning practices to ideate and collaborate on possible solutions. Pitch Perfect emphasizes the importance of teamwork and the practice of self-responsibility, which always offers better and unique results.

WHO WE ARE ...?

We are IEEE Student Branch KIOT. A student-led organization striving to improve active student leadership and gain domain-independent knowledge through conducting technical talks, participating in conferences, and publishing papers. We also help students build teamwork and time management by organizing events in our Student Branch and volunteering in events organized by other Student Branches and various sections within IEEE. There are abundant opportunities to connect with experts and students on an international level and also avail a chance to get mentorship from industry leaders.

Use Cases


Artificial Intelligence

AI - Powered Voice Assistants are set to reach a whopping 8 billion population. AI is a pretty common household term these days. Can you utilize this Big O Brain to solve real world problems?

Big Data and Analytics

Around 2.5 quintillion bytes worth of data are generated each day. Data Scientist and Analysts are the modern day super heroes, creating value out of raw data and supporting organizations in all aspects. Can you become one too?

Cybersecurity and Blockchain

The Holy Triad of CIA - Confidentiality, Integrity and Accessibility. Every secure system must have these attributes. Blockchain is a tech which incorporates all of these concepts to create one unified ledger. What is the best you can spin up?

Clean and sustainable energy

Clean energy has become the measure of development of countries across the world. We stand at the fifth position of this leaderboard. What do you think will make us to overtake China who produce at least 4 times the clean energy that we produce?

Disaster Management

Frequent floods have become a common sight in India. There are lots of lives and resources lost due to lack of efficient mitigation methods and emergency communication systems. Do you think that you might be able to solve that?

Education

Work From Home is pretty sure efficient. But the same can’t be said to online classes, don’t you agree? Cook up a solution to improve interactivity, teaching efficiency and make sure that no kid is distracted away from the screen.

Internet Of Things

A world of connected objects with hyper interactivity at fingertips. Connecting objects, monitoring environments and collecting data - with the help of a swipe of the screen. How can you make the most out of this revolution?

Metaverse

A second world where you can build all, command all and live full on. But what if you can use this godsent gift to help all. Build a solution to solve societal problems across all aspects of life

Phases


PHASE I

Submission of problem statement and abstract on or before 25th September

This submission will be used for shortlisting the participants for the next phase

The teams that are selected for the final round will be announced through mail on 29th September

PHASE II

It will be conducted on 29th September

Each team has to pitch their idea before a panel of judges

Each team will be provided 7 - 10 minutes for their presentation

Judging will be based on innovativeness and feasibility of the presented solution and also on the way the solution is presented

FINAL PHASE

Finalists will present their solutions to a grand panel of jury

Winners will be awarded upto cash prize of Rs. 3000

Rules and Guidelines


This competition is open for all disciplines

Participants can participate individually or in a team of upto 3 members

The problem statement should be related to any one of the mentioned domains

Plagiarizing ideas is discouraged, give your best idea to us without any hesitation

Please make sure to follow the timeline of the event

Decisions taken by the jury will be honest as well as final.
